## Known Limitations
- Numerical parameters show partial convergence in 5 of 30 test cases (17%)
- Historical development of both frameworks not fully addressed
- Taxonomic boundaries between different halakhic categories require further clarification
- Framework specificity to Lurianic Kabbalah (other systems not tested)
- Stronger validation in ritual mitzvot than in ethical imperatives

## Significance
This framework provides the first rigorous mathematical formulation reconciling Maharal's teleological ontology with Lurianic emanational cosmology. The 83% convergence rate with zero falsifications demonstrates that these historically opposed frameworks can be understood as dual coordinate systems within a unified Hilbert space structure. This has profound implications for:
- Jewish philosophical theology
- Philosophy of mathematics and structural realism
- Consciousness studies in religious contexts
- Halakhic decision-making methodology
